About Roadal

Roadal is a map service that provides road users with information about any road activity that might affect road usage. Activities include road contruction, road maintainance, water and sewage projects, and road accidents. Organisations conducting such activities are registered and upload information regarding any projects which might affect road usage. The public can report any activity which might cause a disturbance such as road accidents. The information provided by the public is verified by Roadal officials and if authentic is displayed to the public.
